About TATVA

Tatva is the annual technical fest of BNMIT, which was first organized in the year 2009. Tatva has progressed and evolved to new heights over the last five years, and is now an eagerly awaited event not just at BNMIT but also at colleges across Bengaluru. Tatva 2017 will be the continued endeavor towards reaching a new milestone.
